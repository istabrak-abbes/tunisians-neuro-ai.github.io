TiNA - Tunisians in Neuro-AI

TiNA is a community of Tunisians passionate about the intersection of AI and Neuroscience. We are dedicated to fostering collaborations, showcasing the work of Tunisians, and assisting individuals in joining this field.

About Us

TiNA

Association of Tunisians in Neuro-AI (TiNA) is a community of Tunisians passionate about neuroscience, artificial intelligence, machine learning, cognitive science, and psychiatry. Its members are located worldwide. Read More  

Our Vision

Bring together Tunisians working in the field of Neuro-AI to foster collaborations, promote their work, and support aspiring students joining the field.


Our Mission

Our mission is to make this type of research more accessible through sharing opportunities, expertise, and mentoring.

TiNA components


Together, we bridge the gaps, share knowledge, and uplift each other, no matter where in the world we may be.


Members


TiNA's main objective is to create a collaborative network of Tunisians worldwide. We encourage anyone involved in Neuro-AI to join and participate in our meet-ups and events. Additionally, we welcome individuals who are interested in entering the field and want to join our community.

Learn more »


Meet-ups


Our meet-ups are flexible, with no fixed time or location. We gather wherever there's a community of Tunisian Neuro-AI enthusiasts. Additionally, we plan to host satellite events at Neuro-AI-related conferences.

Projects


As a community, our objective is to foster collaborations, open-source initiatives, and research projects. This may involve bringing diverse members together to explore research topics or developing projects aimed at addressing specific challenges in Tunisia.
